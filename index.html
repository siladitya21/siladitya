Welcome to Our Wedding

About Us

Groom: Siladitya

Siladitya was born and raised in the charming town of Siliguri, hailing from a proud defense family as the only son of Mr. and Mrs. Datta. He completed his schooling and undergraduate studies in Siliguri before pursuing his master’s degree in Kolkata. Presently, he is employed at Utahtechlabs in Kolkata. An avid traveler, Siladitya cherishes the opportunity to explore new horizons, while his family continues to reside in Siliguri.

Bride: Debarati

Debarati, the beloved daughter of Mr. and Mrs. Deb, was born in the vibrant city of Ludhiana, Punjab, and comes from a distinguished defense family. As the elder of two siblings, she has a younger brother who adores her. Currently, Debarati is a dedicated banker at Indian Bank, stationed in Kolkata. A passionate reader, she has recently developed a keen interest in traveling, eager to explore the world beyond the pages of her books. She completed her schooling in Kolkata, where her family now resides in the serene area of Nangi, Budge Budge.

Our Story

One golden afternoon, as the sun cast its gentle glow, Siladitya received a surprise call from Mrs. Deb. Her warm voice carried the affection and curiosity of a mother eager to learn about the young man who had captured her daughter’s heart. Their conversation flowed naturally, filled with laughter and shared thoughts, leaving Mrs. Deb with a comforting sense of connection. By the end, she knew this was someone special. That evening, with a blend of anticipation and excitement, Siladitya and Debarati spoke for the first time. Though brief, their 5-minute exchange held a quiet magic, as if the universe had conspired for their voices to meet. As they spoke, something unspoken lingered in the air—a feeling that this was just the beginning of a beautiful journey. Soon, the families exchanged words, stories, and hopes. One day, Siladitya found himself standing at the threshold of the bride’s home, his heart racing with a mixture of excitement and nerves. When they met, time seemed to slow, as if the world around them had paused to witness their first steps together. Conversations unfolded, smiles exchanged, and hearts quietly began to entwine. After what felt like an eternity of waiting and wondering, the decision was made. In the warmth of family bonds and whispered dreams, the date was set—November 2023, when Siladitya and Debarati would unite their lives in a celebration of love.
